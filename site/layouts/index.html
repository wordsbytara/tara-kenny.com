Hello! I’m Tara Kenny, a culture writer based in New York.

I aim for high brow analysis of “low brow” culture, with an emphasis on how women use and are portrayed in the media. I am the television critic for The Monthly and my writing on film & TV, internet communities, cultural trends and beyond has been published in Interview, i-D, Dazed, The Guardian, Paper, Brooklyn Magazine, Daily Dot, Dirt, Outland, Gossamer, The Saturday Paper, The Age, The Sydney Morning Herald, Frankie and elsewhere.

Additionally, I provide freelance communications, copywriting and grant writing services for businesses and nonprofits. I have a Master’s degree in International Relations from the University of Melbourne and a Bachelor’s degree in Communications from RMIT University.

Follow me @slurpette on IG and reach out to say hello or request my writing portfolio at wordsbytara@gmail.com
